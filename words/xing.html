APRICOT

'杏' represents an 'apricot.' The upper part of '杏' suggests the branches of an apricot tree, symbolizing growth and connection. The lower part resembles the fruit itself, indicating the specific object the character represents. This combination of elements helps convey not just the idea of an apricot, but also its origin and connection to the tree. It's a beautiful way of encapsulating both the physical aspect of the apricot and its natural source in a single character.
