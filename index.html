Welcome to Museum of Remaking Hanzi!

Chinese Hanzi has a huge writing system with 50,000 Hanzi characters, with 10+ different structures that dates back to 3000 years ago. It is one of the most difficult languages to master! But dont run away yet, we will make Hanzi super fun for you! Here you would learn about different structures in Hanzi, their pronunciations and the culture behind through a series of hands-on interactive experiments!
